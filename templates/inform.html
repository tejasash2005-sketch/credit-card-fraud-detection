Credit Card Fraud Detection (Project Report)

Introduction

In today's world, we are on the express train to a cashless society. According to reports, non-cash transactions are growing rapidly every year.

While this growth is impressive, fraudulent transactions are also increasing.

This project builds a Machine Learning Web App to detect such frauds effectively.
